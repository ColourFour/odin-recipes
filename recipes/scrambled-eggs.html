Scrambled Eggs

Description

Simple quick and effective way to scramble eggs

Ingredients

    4 large eggs
    1 pinch salt
    1 pinch pepper
    4 teaspoons butter

Steps

  • Crack eggs and combine with salt and pepper in small bowl
  • Melt butter in skillet on low heat. Whisk eggs while melting
  • Keep skillet on relatively cool. Add whisked eggs to skillet. Let cook for 2 to 3 minutes
  • Remove scrambled eggs before they begin to look dry, they'll continue to cook after they leave the skillet
