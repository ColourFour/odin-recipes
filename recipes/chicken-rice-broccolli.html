Chicken rice and broccoli

Description

Smart, simple meal to hit your macros.

Ingredients

    Extra virgin olive oil
    Chicken breast
    Broccoli
    Rice
    Water
    Seasoning (opitional)

Steps

  • Put rice in rice cooker and let cook.
  • Put olive oil on medium skillet and cook chicken for 4 minutes
  • Remove chicken from skillet. Replace with broccoli. Cook broccoli for 2 minutes.
  • Add finished rice, chicken, and any additional seasoning to skillet and fill the skillet with water.
  • Place lid on skillet and let water boil awayfor 6 minutes.
  • Pour out water. Place oven on high, add tablespoon of olive oil and finish dish.
